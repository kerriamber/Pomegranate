About us

Pomegranate, Inc. was started by a group of computer science students at Stevens Institute of Technology. Going to school next door to the business capital of the world, New York City, inspired the students to beat the smartphone competition by developing a more business savvy phone; a phone that could appeal to the needs of a larger customer base. So the students created the uPhone, a phone that you, the customer, designs to your own personal liking.
